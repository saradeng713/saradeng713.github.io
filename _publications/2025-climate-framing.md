This large-scale cross-national study investigates whether framing climate change messages as losses versus gains differentially affects emotional responses and pro-environmental behaviour. Data were collected across 26 countries. As visiting researcher at the Policy Research Group (University of Cambridge), I contributed to the Chinese cohort — securing 1,908 valid responses in one week — and developed R scripts for the visualisation of primary and secondary outcomes.
