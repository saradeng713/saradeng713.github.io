This paper presents a pre-registered cross-cultural randomised controlled trial evaluating the *iAlive* digital tool, designed to support individuals at risk of suicide. As lead writing and literature analyst for the project, I co-authored the Introduction and Methods sections and led participant recruitment across China and the UK.
